What is Meditation?

Meditation is a skill developed by practicing a few key techniques: mindfulness, focused attention, body scan, visualization, and reflection to name a few.


Practice meditation by gazing at the peaceful waterfall. Letting the gentle sound of the water wash over you, simultaneously removing stress and sending you to a calmer state of mind.
